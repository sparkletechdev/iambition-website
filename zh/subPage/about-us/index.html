Why We Exist ??

We believe that the human mind is built for creativity and not for dealing with technological redundancies. We strive to create a balance between human creativity and machine technology using data. A natural collaboration which will fuel our desire for exploration and problem-solving.

A union which will drive innovation for decades to come. Finding the right data to solve the most challenging problems has always been a driving force of exploration within human nature. This desire has inspired all the great inventions since the beginning of time.

Over the years we have seen how data is used to propel most optimal decisions in the organizations. These organizations have used data as a weapon to stay ahead of the competition, to manage change, to delight customers and to ensure survival regardless of the changing technological landscape.

However, we noticed that the power to bring data-driven innovation is limited to a few organizations. Several reasons contribute to this situation like shortage of people who can effectively utilize the data, heavy infrastructure requirement to utilize the technology and lack of know-how about steering technological changes bought by modern technologies like Big Data, Machine Learning, and Artificial Intelligence.

We decided to go back to the drawing board and think with first principles about how modern organizations can collect, combine, analyze and utilize their data for decision making. We decided to create a platform which will help business leaders exploit the data-driven insights for their decisions without having to worry about all the technical complexities.

Where Are We Going ?

Tuple is being deployed by organizations across the globe for their most critical operations. Businesses are finding their best customers, designing the best customer experiences and developing exciting new products with Tuple.

Financial Services

Financial Services are finding ways to collate their data at a single location so that they have a real-time overview of all the customer activity. It is allowing them to cater to the preferences of their consumer and keep a close watch on the changing digital landscape.

Retailers

Retailers are innovating with the check-out technologies so that their customers can have a hassle-free experience. The industry is looking at modern data-driven technologies to create unique experiences for the consumers and fight the onslaught of e-commerce. They are refining their processes of payments, marketing, and inventory management.

Manufacturing

Manufacturing has not been an early adopter of the technology but they are opening their eyes to the apparent truth. Organizations are automating the way they cater their customer needs by refining the process of quotation management, deploying media listening to generate leads and recommendation engines to optimize lifecycle management of the customers.

Telecom

Telecom sector is creating Intelligent Sales and Marketing engines to enhance the process of customer acquisition, retention, cross-sell and up-sell opportunities.

We are working with leading organizations to prepare them for a future where utilizing the data is a necessity. We are designing products which can withstand digital disruption and keep bringing value to the companies willing to change with time.
